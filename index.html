history

Hingoli was the taluka of Parbhani district and part of Nizam's regime. It was Nizam's military base as it was border place with Vidarbha. In that era military troops, hospitals, veterinary hospital were in operation from Hingoli.


hingoli Established

1 May 1999 This district was carved out from Parbhani district on 1 May 1999 with five own tehsils : Hingoli, Kalamanuri, Sengaon, Aundha Naganath, and Basamat.
